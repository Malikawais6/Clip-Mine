Features

Meaningful Sharing

ClipMine works great for sharing and embedding recorded video-game sessions, tutorials, product reviews, talk shows, speeches, lectures and sports coverage. Try out more samples.

Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the ClipMine experience in your blog or Twitter feed? Just embed our Content-Aware Video Player. It is responsive ad built to work across different modern browsers and devices. Start by tagging a video of choice or pick an already tagged one and simply paste the embed code into your website.

Smart Tagging

Found and interesting point in a video? On ClipMine, adding tags is not reserved for the video owners only - any community members can do it. In addition our smart algorithms can automatically suggest tags (in private beta) for a wide variety of videos.
